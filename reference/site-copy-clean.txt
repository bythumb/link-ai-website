Financing Solutions for Every Need
Beyond traditional home loans, Link offers specialized loans designed to help you access equity, fund renovations, or invest in real estate.
Get Started
Explore Your Loan Options
HELOC (Home Equity Line of Credit)
A HELOC lets you borrow against your home’s equity with a flexible line of credit. Use it for renovations, debt consolidation, or unexpected expenses—only pay interest on what you use.
Reverse Mortgage
For homeowners 62 and older, a reverse mortgage allows you to convert home equity into tax-free cash without monthly mortgage payments. Stay in your home while accessing the funds you need.
VA Loans
VA loans offer zero down payment, lower interest rates, and no private mortgage insurance (PMI) for eligible veterans and active-duty service members—making homeownership more accessible.
FHA Loans
Designed for first-time buyers and those with lower credit scores, FHA loans require as little as 3.5% down and offer more flexible qualification criteria than conventional loans.
Construction Loans
Financing designed for new construction, a construction loan gives you the funds to build while offering flexible draw schedules and interest-only payments during the build phase.
Renovation Loans
Finance home improvements with a loan designed for renovations, covering everything from minor upgrades to full remodels—all with competitive rates and simple terms.
DSCR Loans (Debt-Service Coverage Ratio)
A DSCR loan is tailored for real estate investors looking to qualify based on rental income rather than personal income—perfect for expanding your portfolio with flexible terms.
Bridge Loans
Need to buy a new home before selling your current one? A bridge loan provides temporary financing so you can move forward without delays.
